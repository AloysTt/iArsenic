You need to carry out a chemical test of your well water to be sure. ____________It is estimated that ~60% of 10 million hand pumped tubewells in rural Bangladesh remained untested for arsenic. This accounts for widespread exposure to this insidious toxin and resulting in multi-systemic effects including cancer. Unfortunately, there is no specific treatment but drinking arsenic-safe water. Using this web application a tubewell can easily be assessed for arsenic. All you need to provide your Upazila name (which you can find in the drop-down list), colour of the tubewell-platform (see 'Platform Staining Guide' tab for examples), and select a depth of tubewell. Please also tell us whether the tubewell water is used for drinking; then press 'Assess' button and then see the outcome in 'Our Assessment' tab.
